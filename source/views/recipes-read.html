Nutrition

Ingredients

  • Thinly-sliced apples: 6 cups
  • White sugar: 3/4 cup

Preparation Instructions

Cut and peel the apples
Try not to cut too deep into the apple as you don't want to unneccesarily waste any of it!
Mix the sugar with the cinnamon
Use a regular metal spoon in order to easily cut through the mix.
Use additional sugar for tart apples.
Chop the apples
Ensure the apples are evenly-sliced cubes no larger than one centimer across so that the various cubes cook evenly.

Cooking Directions

  1. Firstly, ensure that you are thoroughly prepared by following the Preparation Instructions.
  2. Preheat your over to 200 degrees celsius.
  3. Place the pot into the over on the top shelf, ensuring that there is a gap of around 6 centimeters above the pot to allow the pie to rise.
